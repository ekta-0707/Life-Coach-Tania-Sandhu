About Tania

Hi, I’m Tania Arora — a life coach, behavioral consultant, and soul on a mission.

I help people reconnect with their true selves—beyond pressure and expectations. From guiding neurodiverse children to coaching adults through mindset shifts, my mission is to empower you to live with clarity, confidence, and purpose.

Born in India, now in Canada, my work blends compassion, cultural insight, and conscious transformation. Whether through 1:1 coaching, my podcast Pilgrimage with Tia, or my book Brown Boundaries, I’m here to support your journey home—to yourself.
